Descriptor-In-Pixel: Point-Feature Tracking for Pixel Processor Arrays

Laurie Bose, Jianing Chen, Piotr Dudek
University Of Manchester
CVPR 2025
Paper CVPR 2025 Page

Point-Feature detection and tracking at thousands of frames-per-second, using ~1Watt of power. All computation is performed inside the sensor itself, upon thousands of "Pixel-Procesors".
